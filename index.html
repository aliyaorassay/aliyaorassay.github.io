The general information about Oral

Oral known in Russian as Uralsk, is a city in northwestern Kazakhstan, at the confluence of the Ural and Chagan rivers close to the Russian border. As it is located on the western bank of the Ural river, it is considered geographically in Europe. It is the capital of the West Kazakhstan Region. The ethnic composition is dominated by Kazakhs (71%) and Russians (25%).

Get to know a history

Uralsk is the oldest city in what we now call Kazakhstan. It was established by Cossacks in 1584 as Yaitskiy Gorodok, a border outpost of the Russian empire at the strategic confluence of two rivers: the Ural, then called the Yaik, and the Chagan
